AI Energy Score Ratings

The AI Energy Score project provides standardized benchmarks to measure energy consumption across AI models, helping developers make informed, sustainable choices.

The AI Energy Score is a step toward industry-wide adoption of energy efficiency benchmarks, encouraging sustainable practices in AI development.
